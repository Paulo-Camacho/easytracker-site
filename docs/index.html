FAQ

How do I delete my account?
Open the app → hamburger menu (top-left) → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all your data. It cannot be undone.
How does the AI food estimator work?
Describe your meal in plain text or snap a photo. The AI returns calorie, protein, carb, and fat estimates. Estimates are approximate — use the barcode scanner or manual entry for packaged foods.
What is Pro?
Free tier includes unlimited manual logging, barcode scanning, and gym tracking. Pro unlocks unlimited AI text and photo macro estimates.
How do I add friends?
Go to the Friends tab and share your 6-character code. Have them enter it on their end to connect. The social feed is friends-only.
I forgot my gamertag / want to change it.
Gamertag changes aren't supported yet. Email us and we'll handle it manually.
My streak reset but I logged every day.
Streaks require at least one food entry before midnight in your local timezone. If yours reset unexpectedly, email us with the affected dates.
